Selection Criteria

Who We Accept

The 100 positions are not filled on the basis of fame, following, or professional seniority alone. They are filled on the basis of idea quality, intellectual readiness, and the potential for lasting contribution.

We are looking for
  • A genuinely original idea worth sharing
  • A perspective shaped by authentic African context
  • Professional credibility and lived experience
  • Willingness to invest in the preparation process
  • Commitment to the full five-day experience
Backgrounds we welcome
  • Policy and governance
  • Technology and innovation
  • Finance and enterprise
  • Arts, culture, and creative industries
  • Science, climate, and development
