A Message from the Team Lead

Project TEDxAfrica was founded on the belief that the world's most transformative opportunities emerge when exceptional people, ideas, and cultures come together with intention.

More than a licensed TEDx experience, we are an independently curated, non-profit ecosystem where global leaders engage Africa not as spectators, but as collaborators, sharing knowledge, experiencing the continent's rich cultural heritage, building trusted relationships, and contributing to conversations that shape the future. We are creating a platform where influence is earned through meaningful exchange, and where every encounter has the potential to become a lifelong partnership.

Our invitation is reserved for those who seek more than a speaking engagement or another conference.

It is for individuals who believe that legacy is built through contribution, that cultural understanding is as valuable as professional achievement, and that the most enduring impact comes from the relationships we cultivate along the way.

If you are ready to experience Africa with purpose, connect with remarkable people, and become part of an institution committed to ideas, excellence, and lasting global collaboration,

I warmly welcome you to Project TEDxAfrica.

Our Philosophy

Built on Three Principles

We believe enduring influence is never accidental. Every Project TEDxAfrica experience is intentionally designed around three principles that transform expertise into lasting impact.

01

Ideas Before Applause

The world's most influential talks are not remembered because they were delivered brilliantly. They are remembered because they changed the way people think. We help you refine an idea that is clear, credible, and capable of creating meaningful impact long after you leave the stage.

02

Influence Through Connection

Great opportunities are built on meaningful relationships. Beyond the TEDx stage, we intentionally create opportunities for executive engagement, strategic partnerships, and authentic cross-cultural collaboration that extend your influence far beyond a single presentation.

03

Legacy Beyond the Stage

A TEDx talk is not the destination. It is the beginning of a much larger journey. Our goal is to help position your ideas, expertise, and leadership within conversations that continue to create value for years to come.
