Our Approach

The Speaker Development Programme

Every exceptional idea deserves exceptional preparation. At Project TEDxAfrica, we believe that a defining TEDx talk is the product of disciplined thinking, rigorous refinement, and purposeful execution. Our Speaker Development Programme is designed for accomplished leaders whose ideas have the potential to influence industries, institutions, and societies.

Working alongside our advisory faculty, each speaker embarks on a structured journey that transforms expertise into a compelling idea worthy of one of the world's most respected platforms.

This is not presentation training. It is an executive development experience that strengthens intellectual clarity, sharpens strategic communication, and prepares leaders to deliver ideas with confidence, credibility, and lasting impact.

Phase One
Intellectual Clarity

Every remarkable talk begins with a single, powerful idea. Together, we identify, challenge, and refine the central insight that will define your message and distinguish your contribution.

Phase Two
Narrative Design

The most influential ideas are both intellectually rigorous and deeply engaging. We develop a narrative that combines clarity, evidence, and authentic storytelling to create an experience audiences remember.

Phase Three
Executive Presence

A powerful idea deserves an equally compelling delivery. Through personalized preparation, speakers cultivate the presence, confidence, and precision expected on a stage recognized around the world.

The Process

The Speaker Development Journey

A world-class preparation experience. Every Project TEDxAfrica speaker embarks on a carefully structured development journey designed to produce talks of exceptional quality and lasting significance. From the moment you are accepted, you are supported by an experienced advisory faculty committed to refining your ideas, strengthening your message, and preparing you for one of the world's most respected platforms.

Acceptance
Onboarding & Strategic Alignment

Your journey begins with a comprehensive orientation, introduction to your Advisory Faculty member, and a strategic assessment of your proposed idea. Together, we establish the foundation for a talk capable of creating lasting influence.

Discovery
Idea Development

Through a series of personalised advisory sessions, your central idea is challenged, refined, and strengthened until it is clear, original, and intellectually compelling.

Development
Narrative & Talk Design

Working collaboratively with your advisor, your expertise is transformed into a compelling TEDx narrative — one that combines clarity, credibility, evidence, and emotional resonance while remaining authentic to your voice.

Refinement
Executive Rehearsals

Multiple rehearsal sessions prepare you for the demands of the TEDx stage. Every aspect of your delivery, from presence and pacing to storytelling and audience engagement, is refined with precision.

Arrival
The Executive Speaker Summit

Before stepping onto the red circle, speakers gather in Africa for an exclusive preparation experience featuring live rehearsals, collaborative peer review, final refinements, and executive networking. This final stage ensures every speaker arrives at the main event prepared, confident, and ready to deliver an idea that can resonate far beyond the stage.
