A Message From Our Team Lead: Why We Built This Platform

On influence, contribution, and what happens after the stage.

Izuchukwu Ukandu
Team Lead, Project TEDxAfrica

Project TEDxAfrica was founded on the belief that the world's most transformative opportunities emerge when exceptional people, ideas, and cultures come together with intention.

More than a licensed TEDx experience, we are an independently curated, non-profit ecosystem where global leaders engage Africa not as spectators, but as collaborators — sharing knowledge, experiencing the continent's rich cultural heritage, building trusted relationships, and contributing to conversations that shape the future. We are creating a platform where influence is earned through meaningful exchange, and where every encounter has the potential to become a lifelong partnership.

Our invitation is reserved for those who seek more than a speaking engagement or another conference. It is for individuals who believe that legacy is built through contribution, that cultural understanding is as valuable as professional achievement, and that the most enduring impact comes from the relationships we cultivate along the way.

If you are ready to experience Africa with purpose, connect with remarkable people, and become part of an institution committed to ideas, excellence, and lasting global collaboration, I warmly welcome you to Project TEDxAfrica.
