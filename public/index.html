The Case for Africa

Why Africa?
Why Now?

Africa is not a problem to be solved. It is a proposition to be understood. By 2040, the continent will house the world's largest working-age population. The question is not whether Africa will shape global affairs — it is whether the right voices will be positioned to lead when that moment arrives.

Project TEDxAfrica exists to ensure those voices are prepared, visible, and globally credible before the decade demands them.

Our Philosophy

Built on Three Pillars

Every element of the TEDxAfrica platform is constructed around three irreducible principles.

01

Story

Your narrative is the most powerful instrument of leadership. We help you find the precise articulation of your idea that will move the world.

02

Identity

Africa's future leaders must carry the full weight of their context — cultural, historical, and geo-political — without apology or compromise.

03

Legacy

A TEDx talk is not a performance. It is a permanent contribution to the global record. We prepare you for the weight and permanence of that responsibility.
