Project TEDxAfrica offers a limited number of strategic partnership positions to organisations whose work aligns with the vision of advancing Africa's leadership on the global stage.

Partnership Types

We Work With
Three Types of Partners

Government

Government & Institutional

Ministries, development agencies, national commissions, and intergovernmental bodies whose mandates align with Africa's leadership, economic, or cultural development. Government partners shape the institutional weight of the event.

  • Featured in programme materials
  • Delegate access and reserved seating
  • Inclusion in Day 3 institutional programme
NGO / Civil Society

Non-Governmental & Development

Foundations, international NGOs, development finance institutions, and civil society organisations working at the intersection of ideas, leadership, and African development. NGO partners bring thematic depth and international credibility.

  • Co-branded content and reporting
  • Thematic session naming rights
  • Speaker nomination pathway
Corporate

Corporate & Enterprise

Pan-African businesses, multinational corporations with African operations, and high-growth enterprises aligned with continental economic leadership. Corporate partners gain brand association with the most credible leadership platform on the continent.

  • Stage and environment branding
  • Executive table at closing reception
  • Digital and broadcast exposure
